Group Health Insurance Policy

This policy comes under the Employee Benefit Insurance schemes which are meant for welfare and wellbeing of employees. This policy provides social security benefits which include compensation for hospitalisation, untimely death, disabilities, occupational diseases and injuries.

  • Introduction
  • Who can take this Policy?
  • Coverage
  • Add on Covers
  • Basis of Sum lnsured
  • Exclusions

Group Health Insurance Policy (GHI) is taken by employers to cover their employees and family members. The coverage under the policy is uniform for all employees/ members of the group except when sum insured vary for employees. Fabulous benefits are available in the policy at very competitive premium rates.

An organization can take this policy for their onroll employees

Coverage under Group Health Insurance Policy are as follows: 

  • Self, Spouse, 2 children and Parents can be covered with or without Family Floater. 
  • Room Rent and ICU expenses
  • Ambulance Charges
  • Maternity Benefit up to Rs.50,000 per claim with or without 9 month waiting period
  • Newborn Baby Coverage from Day One within Maternity Sum Insured 
  • Pre-existing diseases 
  • Waiver of 30 day waiting period
  • Waiver of 1st, 2nd & 4th  year exclusions
  • Corporate Floater for major illness
  • Domiciliary benefit 
  • OPD charges
  • Expenses incurred 30 days before and 60 days after hospitalization

Add-on covers under Group Health Insurance Policy are as follows: 

  • Pre Existing Disease 
  • Room Rent Waiver 
  • Maternity Cover
  • Daily Hospital Cash
  • Corporate buffer
     

Sum insured of INR 50,000 - 10 Lac per employee/family member can be taken.

Exclusion under Group Health Insurance Policy are as follows: 

  • Addictive conditions or alcohol/drug abuse
  • Alternative Treatment
  • Treatment outside the geographical limits
  • War
  • Nuclear Risk
  • Cosmetic Treatment
  • Sleep Disorders

Important Points to Remember While Buying the Policy

Group policy sync with individual policy to get the continuation benefit of exit employees

  • A Clause can be added in Group Policy wherein the group benefit can be synchronized with Individual Policy for the employees exiting the organization and taking the individual policy from the same Insurer.
  • A Clause can be added in Group Policy wherein the group benefit can be synchronized with Individual Policy for the employees exiting the organization and taking the individual policy from the same Insurer.

Corporate Floater can be covered

  • Corporate Floater provides facility to cover the medical expenses incase of individual familiy sum insured exhausted.
  • Corporate Floater provides facility to cover the medical expenses incase of individual familiy sum insured exhausted.

Parents can be covered along with employees

  • GMC Policy provides an option to cover employee's parents as well by charging additional premium.
  • This option nulify the requirement of separate health policy for parents.

Key Documents at The Time Of Claims

General Claim Intimation Format

The first step is to intimate the insurers with the following information along with the claim intimation:

  • Name of the Patient
  • Patient’s Health ID Card Number
  • Hospital Name
  • Hospital address with contact No etc
  • Date of admission
  • Illness / disease for which hospitalised and treatment details.

In case of an accident, below mentioned information needs to be provided:

  • Date & time of accident / loss
  • Brief description of accident along with place of accident
  • Name and address of police station if FIR filed
  • Name of person who took insured to hospital (if possible)

Which are the important rating factors for GMC Policy ?

Important rating factor includes:

  • Family definition
  • Average age of members
  • Sum insured
  • Add on covers
  • Claim ratio of expiring policy (in renewal cases only)
Do i have to pay any amount on behalf of my employees?

The decision to pay the premium of the GMC policy, either partly or completely, rests solely on the employer. If the employer wants, he can pay the premiums in full on behalf of his employees. Contrary to this, the employer might also ask his employees to bear the entire premium themselves or pay the premium partly.

How does claim amount in the expiring policy impact the premium?

Claim history reveals the risk exposure of the group, whether the health of the lives in the group is standard or sub-standard.

Can i reduce my coverage to reduce the overall premium?

Yes, group mediclaim policy are highly customizable. You can take the coverage based on your need and preference.

what is the impact of co-payment benefit on premium under the policy?

Co-payment benefit overall reduces the burden of premium on the employer. The claim amount is shared by the claimant upto the co-payment limit specified in the policy.

What is covered in GMC policy?

Hospitalization expenses such as room charges, nursing care, cost of medicines etc. is covered in the policy.

Who can buy GMC Policy?

GMC Policy is purchased by employers to cover medical expenses of their employees, dependents like spouse, children and parents.

What is the smallest size of group which qualifies to be benefitted by GMC policy?

As per exposure draft dt. 19-1-2016 of IRDA, minimum number of persons required to form a group for purpose of a group health insurance policy is 20. However, group policy under micro insurance can be issued to 5 members.

What are additional benefits available under GMC Policy?

Pre-existing diseases and maternity expenses can be covered. Waiting period of 30 day & 1 year are also waived.

Can pre existing be covered from day one under Group Mediclaim Policy ?

Yes, Pre existing disease can be covered from day one under Group Mediclaim Policy.

Is claim payable if treatment is not taken in network hospital?

Expenses are reimbursed subject to limits specified in the policy. The insured has to fill up claim form and produce allmedical bills along with prescriptions, test reports.

In case of a major operation if limit of expenditure of a group member gets exhausted, what should the member do?

Amount can be sanctioned from Corporate Buffer for named operations/procedures.

Can a group member claim beyond individual sum insured or floater sum insured?

No one individual or family can't claim beyond the Family Sum Insured

How reimbursement claims are settled ?

Under reimbursement claims all original documents duly signed by the doctor along with Claim Bill are required along with the reason why cashless was not opted.

Who decides whether Corporate buffer to be utilized at the time when limit of an employee exhausted in a policy year ?

It is generally decided by Human Resource Department of the Company.

Are medical tests compulsory for GMC Policy?

No Medical test is conducted under Group Policy unless any insured member is of more age than the limit specified

Are Group Health policies tax deductibles?

Only employer can claim the premium paid for the group insurance policy as a tax deduction., An employee is not entitled to IT exemption under the health cover provided by an employer.

What are the advantages of Group Health Insurance for an employee ?

Advantages of Group Health Insurance for an Employee is as follows: 
• Pre Existing Disease is covered from Day 1 
• An Extensive range of coverage with no limit on diseases
• Larger Maternity Coverage

What are the advantages of Group Health Insurance for an employer ?

Advantages of Group Health Insurance for an Employer is as follows: 
• Helps in Employee Retention
• Tax Benefit
• Motivated Employees
• Better Benefits in Lower Costs
